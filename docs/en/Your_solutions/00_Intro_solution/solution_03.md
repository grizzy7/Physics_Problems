### Solving a System of Equations

Given the equations:

1. 2x + 3y = 12  
2. x − y = 1  

Step 1: Solve the second equation for x

x − y = 1  

x = y + 1

Step 2: Substitute x into the first equation

2(y + 1) + 3y = 12

Step 3: Simplify

2y + 2 + 3y = 12  

5y + 2 = 12

Step 4: Solve for y

5y = 10  

y = 2

Step 5: Substitute y back to find x

x = y + 1  

x = 2 + 1  

x = 3

### Final Answer

x = 3  
y = 2
