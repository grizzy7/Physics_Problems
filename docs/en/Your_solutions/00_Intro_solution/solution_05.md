### Vector Components

A vector **A** has a magnitude of **15** and makes an angle of **θ = 60°** with the horizontal axis.

To find the components:

Horizontal component:
Aₓ = A cos(θ)

Vertical component:
Aᵧ = A sin(θ)

### Step 1: Horizontal Component

Aₓ = 15 cos(60°)

cos(60°) = 0.5

Aₓ = 15 × 0.5  
Aₓ = 7.5

### Step 2: Vertical Component

Aᵧ = 15 sin(60°)

sin(60°) ≈ 0.866

Aᵧ = 15 × 0.866  
Aᵧ ≈ 12.99

### Final Answer

Horizontal component: **Aₓ = 7.5**  
Vertical component: **Aᵧ ≈ 12.99**
