### Rearranging the Pendulum Formula to Solve for g

Start with the formula for the period of a simple pendulum:

T = 2π√(L / g)

Step 1: Square both sides to remove the square root

T² = (2π)²(L / g)

T² = 4π²(L / g)

Step 2: Solve for g

Multiply both sides by g:

gT² = 4π²L

Now divide both sides by T²:

g = (4π²L) / T²

### Final Formula

g = (4π²L) / T²
