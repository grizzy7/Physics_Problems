1. Wave Properties
A sound wave in air has a frequency of 440 Hz. If the speed of sound in air is 343 m/s, what is its wavelength? What is its wavelength in water, where the speed of sound is 1482 m/s?






To find the wavelength ($\lambda$), use the formula: $\lambda = \frac{v}{f}$

**1. Wavelength in Air**
$$v = 343 \text{ m/s}$$
$$f = 440 \text{ Hz}$$
$$\lambda = \frac{343}{440} \approx \mathbf{0.780 \text{ m}}$$



**2. Wavelength in Water**
$$v = 1482 \text{ m/s}$$
$$f = 440 \text{ Hz}$$
$$\lambda = \frac{1482}{440} \approx \mathbf{3.368 \text{ m}}$$
