


### 1. Identify the Given Information
* **Speed of sound ($v$):** $343 \text{ m/s}$
* **Total time ($t$):** $1 \text{ s}$ (This is the time for the "round trip")

### 2. The Formula
The total distance traveled by the sound is calculated using the formula:
$$\text{Total Distance} = \text{Speed} \times \text{Time}$$

However, because the sound travels to the cliff and back, the distance to the cliff ($d$) is exactly half of the total distance traveled:
$$d = \frac{v \times t}{2}$$

### 3. Calculation
Substitute the values into the formula:
$$d = \frac{343 \text{ m/s} \times 1 \text{ s}}{2}$$
$$d = \frac{343 \text{ m}}{2}$$
$$d = 171.5 \text{ m}$$

**The cliff is $171.5 \text{ meters}$ away.**
