Given:
* $L = 64 \text{ cm} = 0.64 \text{ m}$
* $f = 330 \text{ Hz}$
* Harmonic: Fundamental ($n = 1$)

Formulas:
* $\lambda = 2L$
* $v = f \lambda$

Calculation:
$$\lambda = 2 \times 0.64 \text{ m} = 1.28 \text{ m}$$
$$v = 330 \text{ Hz} \times 1.28 \text{ m} = 422.4 \text{ m/s}$$

**Wave Speed:** $422.4 \text{ m/s}$
