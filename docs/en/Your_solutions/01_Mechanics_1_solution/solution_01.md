
# Projectile Motion Solution

A projectile is fired with an initial velocity of **100 m/s** at an angle of **37°** above the horizontal.  
Assume **no air resistance** and take **g = 10 m/s²**.

---

## Step 1: Resolve Velocity into Components

- Horizontal velocity:
  vₓ = v cosθ = 100 × 0.8 = **80 m/s**

- Vertical velocity:
  vᵧ = v sinθ = 100 × 0.6 = **60 m/s**

---

## Step 2: Time of Flight

Formula:
T = 2vᵧ / g

Calculation:
T = (2 × 60) / 10 = **12 s**

---

## Step 3: Maximum Height

Formula:
H = vᵧ² / (2g)

Calculation:
H = 60² / (2 × 10)  
H = 3600 / 20 = **180 m**

---

## Step 4: Range

Formula:
R = vₓ × T

Calculation:
R = 80 × 12 = **960 m**

---

## Final Answers

- Time of flight = **12 s**
- Maximum height = **180 m**
- Range = **960 m**
