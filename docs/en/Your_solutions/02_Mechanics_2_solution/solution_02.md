### 1. Identifying the Parameters
Given: $x(t) = 0.2 \cos(10\pi t)$
Comparing to $x(t) = A \cos(\omega t)$:
- $A = 0.2 \text{ m}$
- $\omega = 10\pi \text{ rad/s}$
- $m = 10 \text{ kg}$

### 2. Spring Constant ($k$)
Using $k = m\omega^2$:
$$k = 10 \cdot (10\pi)^2 = 1000\pi^2 \text{ N/m}$$
$$k \approx 9869.60 \text{ N/m}$$

### 3. Total Mechanical Energy ($E$)
Using $E = \frac{1}{2} k A^2$:
$$E = \frac{1}{2} (1000\pi^2) (0.2)^2 = 20\pi^2 \text{ J}$$
$$E \approx 197.39 \text{ J}$$
