### 1. Known Constants and Values
* **Distance ($r$):** $5.3 \times 10^{-11} \text{ m}$
* **Charges:** $q_p = +1.60 \times 10^{-19} \text{ C}$, $q_e = -1.60 \times 10^{-19} \text{ C}$
* **Masses:** $m_p = 1.67 \times 10^{-27} \text{ kg}$, $m_e = 9.11 \times 10^{-31} \text{ kg}$
* **Constants:** $k = 8.99 \times 10^9 \text{ N}\cdot\text{m}^2/\text{C}^2$, $G = 6.67 \times 10^{-11} \text{ N}\cdot\text{m}^2/\text{kg}^2$

---

### 2. Calculations

**Electric Force ($F_e$):**

$$F_e = k \frac{|q_p q_e|}{r^2}$$

$$F_e = (8.99 \times 10^9) \frac{(1.6 \times 10^{-19})^2}{(5.3 \times 10^{-11})^2}$$

$$F_e \approx 8.2 \times 10^{-8} \text{ N}$$

**Gravitational Force ($F_g$):**

$$F_g = G \frac{m_p m_e}{r^2}$$

$$F_g = (6.67 \times 10^{-11}) \frac{(1.67 \times 10^{-27})(9.11 \times 10^{-31})}{(5.3 \times 10^{-11})^2}$$

$$F_g \approx 3.6 \times 10^{-47} \text{ N}$$

**The Ratio ($F_e / F_g$):**

$$\text{Ratio} = \frac{8.2 \times 10^{-8}}{3.6 \times 10^{-47}} \approx 2.3 \times 10^{39}$$
